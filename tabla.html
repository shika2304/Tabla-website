The Guru

Training by Pamela Banerjee

Pamela Banerjee is a gold medallist tabla artist and the guiding hand behind Ragranjani Music Academy in Belgharia, Nimta, Kolkata. She holds an M.A. in Percussion (Tabla) — First Class First — from Rabindra Bharati University, and is a National Scholar awarded by the Ministry of Culture, Government of India.

A disciple of Pt. Aninda Chatterjee, Pt. Dilip Mukherjee, and Pt. Asish Sengupta, her teaching carries the full weight of the guru–shishya tradition — precise, patient, and deeply rooted in taal.

  • National Scholar — Ministry of Culture, Govt of India
  • M.A. Percussion (Tabla), 1st Class 1st & Gold Medalist — Rabindra Bharati University
  • 1st position — Murari Smriti Tabla Solo (3 times)
  • 1st position — All Bengal Tabla Lahara Competition (2 times)
  • 1st position — Sarba Varatiya Sangeet O Sanskriti Parishad
  • 2nd position — Inter Division Railways Cultural Competition
  • 3rd position — Doverlane Music Competition, Tabla Solo
Ragranjani Music Academy is affiliated with Sura Bharati Sangeet Kala Kendra.

Curriculum

What You Will Learn

Four pillars of training, taught step by step — online and offline.

Basic & Advanced Method of Tabla Teaching

A structured path from your first bols to advanced playing, building clean technique and confident hands at every stage.

Knowledge of Taal

Understand the rhythmic cycles at the heart of Indian classical music — their structure, theka, and feel.

Accompaniment for Vocal Music & Dance

Proper guidance of accompaniment for all types of vocal music and dance — learning to listen, support, and shine.

Training for Beginners & Interested Learners

No prior experience needed — every sincere learner is welcomed and guided from the very first lesson.
