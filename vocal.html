The Guru

Training by Nirmalya Sen

Nirmalya Sen is a vocalist and music teacher at Ragranjani Music Academy in Belgharia, Kolkata. With deep roots in the classical and devotional traditions of Indian music, his teaching nurtures each student’s voice with patience, discipline, and genuine warmth.

Training spans a wide range of musical forms — from the rigour of Indian Classical to the lyrical grace of Rabindra Sangeet, the fervour of Nazrul Geeti, the intimacy of Ghazals, the vibrancy of Modern Bengali Songs, and the devotion of Bhajans. Students also receive training in instrumental accompaniment, and annual examinations are arranged for all.

Ragranjani Music Academy is affiliated with Sura Bharati Sangeet Kala Kendra.

Curriculum

What You Will Learn

A complete musical education — classical discipline, lyrical tradition, and devotional depth.

Indian Classical Music

Vocal training rooted in the raga tradition — swara, alankar, and the foundations of Hindustani or Carnatic practice.

Rabindra Sangeet

The songs of Rabindranath Tagore — their melodic subtlety, emotional breadth, and characteristic phrasing.

Nazrul Geeti

The passionate songs of Kazi Nazrul Islam — patriotic, lyrical, and deeply rooted in the Bengali musical soul.

Ghazals

The poetic Urdu lyric form — trained in its intricate metre, longing, and the art of expressive delivery.

Modern Bengali Songs

Contemporary Bengali music — adhunik gaan with modern arrangements, nuanced expression, and popular appeal.

Bhajans & Devotional Songs

Sacred songs from across traditions — cultivating the meditative, surrendered quality that devotional music demands.

Training also covers various other forms of music with instrumental accompaniment, guided by the needs of each student.
